Features

Everything You Need to Save a Life

A complete platform designed for both patients in need and verified medical organisations managing their blood stock.

Real-Time Location Search

Use GPS or type your city — our system finds blood banks within your proximity with accurate distance data.

Verified Institutions Only

Every blood bank goes through a strict verification process. Only government-licensed, identity-verified organisations appear in results.

Live Stock Updates

Blood banks update their inventory directly via their dashboard. You always see the latest available units — no outdated data.

Urgent Mode Priority

Mark your search as Urgent. Results are sorted to show closest, highest-stock banks first to minimise emergency response time.

Google Maps Integration

One-click directions to any blood bank using Google Maps. No copy-pasting addresses — direct navigation support built in.

Dual Dashboard System

Separate, role-based dashboards for patients/users and blood bank organisations. Each sees exactly what they need, nothing more.
