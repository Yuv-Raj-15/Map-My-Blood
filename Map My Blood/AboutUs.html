About Map My Blood

A verified blood discovery and direction platform that helps users find nearby blood availability instantly, while enabling blood banks to maintain real-time stock data safely and transparently.

Our Mission

What is Map My Blood?

Map My Blood is a digital support platform built for India — connecting blood seekers, verified blood banks, and hospitals. A user enters location, blood group, and urgency level. Our system filters nearby availability and displays matching results with map-based directions.

The platform is accessible to both public users and medical stakeholders. Separate dashboards ensure role-based access: users search and request information, while blood banks manage stock updates. Only original and verified institutions are shown in search results. Built by college students as a free public service — because every second in an emergency counts.

Process

How the Platform Works

User Input

Users provide location, required blood type, and urgency status (Normal / High / Critical).

Database Sorting

Our system fetches nearby blood units by type and priority from verified sources only.

Map + Direction

Results include branch details, distance, contact info, and one-click Google Maps directions.

Live Inventory Control

Only verified blood banks and hospitals can update their own blood stock via their secure dashboard.

Registration

Registration Criteria

For Users

  • Valid mobile number and email verification
  • Basic location permission for nearby search and route assistance
  • Acceptance of terms, privacy policy, and emergency disclaimer

For Blood Banks / Hospitals

  • Government registration certificate and operating license
  • Authorized representative ID proof and institution verification documents
  • Branch address validation and emergency contact submission
  • Approval process by platform verification team before dashboard activation

Note: Only original and verified blood banks are published in public search results.

Legal

Terms & Conditions (Summary)

  • Platform provides location and availability support; final confirmation must be done by direct call to the blood bank.
  • Stock can change rapidly in emergencies; displayed units are based on latest updates by institutions.
  • Any misuse, fake listing, or manipulated stock entry can lead to immediate account suspension.
  • Users must not submit false emergency requests or misuse institution contact numbers.
  • Hospitals and blood banks are solely responsible for accuracy of their own inventory entries.

Privacy
